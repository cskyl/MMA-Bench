Some Modalities are More Equal Than Others

Decoding and Architecting Multimodal Integration in MLLMs

We introduce MMA-Bench to probe how modern multimodal LLMs behave when sight, sound, and language conflict. Using both black-box and white-box analysis, we expose brittle modality integration and show how alignment-aware tuning leads to stronger cross-modal grounding and more reliable reasoning. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

  • Benchmark controlled audio–video–text conflicts and unimodal ablations.
  • Diagnose modality bias via performance and attention patterns.
  • Teach models when to prioritize, leverage, or ignore specific modalities.
  • Move towards MLLMs with intrinsically reliable cross-modal reasoning.
