Some Modalities are More Equal Than Others

Decoding and architecting reliable multimodal integration in large language models.

MMA-Bench is a benchmark and analysis suite for stress-testing audio–video–text reasoning. We combine controlled misalignment setups, black-box evaluation, and white-box attention analysis to understand when models over-trust, under-use, or correctly balance different modalities.

  • Probe modality bias with aligned vs. misaligned audio-video-text scenarios.
  • Analyze layer- and head-wise attention to reveal where fusion actually happens.
  • Design training recipes that encourage robust cross-modal reasoning.
